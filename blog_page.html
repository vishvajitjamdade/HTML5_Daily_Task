Laziness is a primary enemy of success

Vishvajit Jamdade


28 March 2026

Laziness is one of the biggest obstacles to success. It makes us delay our work and waste valuable time. When we avoid effort, we miss opportunities to learn and grow. Success requires consistency, discipline, and hard work. By overcoming laziness and staying focused on our goals, we can achieve great things in life.
